About OnChain Bazaar

OnChain Bazaar is a decentralized e-commerce platform that empowers local businesses by giving them access to broader markets. Our mission is to make buying and selling more accessible by providing a platform where vendors can list products, accept payments in cryptocurrency or through local payment methods like M-Pesa, and connect with customers locally and globally.

Empowering Local Businesses

We focus on giving small businesses the tools they need to succeed in the modern digital economy by offering them a platform to reach new customers and grow their sales using on-chain technology.

Seamless Payments

By integrating M-Pesa and cryptocurrency payments, we provide secure, fast, and affordable payment options for both buyers and sellers, whether they are local or international.

Blockchain Security

All transactions are secure and transparent, thanks to blockchain technology. Buyers and sellers can interact with confidence, knowing their payments are protected and tracked on-chain.
